### 2. Update Social Media Links (Takes 3 minutes)
Find lines 67-80 in `index.html`:

Replace the `#` with your actual social media URLs:
- GitHub: `https://github.com/yourusername`
- LinkedIn: `https://linkedin.com/in/yourusername`
- Twitter: `https://twitter.com/yourusername`
- Dribbble: `https://dribbble.com/yourusername`

Find lines 135-155 in `index.html` and write your own introduction

### 4. Add Your Projects (Takes 10 minutes)
Find the Projects section (lines 340-475) and:
- Replace placeholder images with your project screenshots
- Update project titles and descriptions
- Add your GitHub repo links and live demo URLs
